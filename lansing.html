Lansing: The Capital of Michigan

Here are some facts about the city of Lansing.

City Pop. in 2020 was 114,297.(Britannica)

The city of Lansing was incorporated in 1859.(Britannica)

Lansing is located in Ingham county in the middle of mid michigan
on the Grand River at its junction with the Red Cedar River.(Britannica)

The city of Lansing is considered an urban enviroment (Wikipedia)

The median income in Lansing for a houshold was $77,745 according to the 2022 data.
This is above the state median income of $66,167 at that same time (Lansing, Michigan)
