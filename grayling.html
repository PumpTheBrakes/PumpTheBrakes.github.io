City of Grayling Michigan

Here are some facts about the city of Grayling.

City Pop. in 2022 was 1,758.(U.S. Census Bureau)

The city of Grayling resides in Crawford county. The governing body for the county formed in 1879(crawford county Michigan history)

Grayling is in the middle of the northern Michigan region at the junctions of intersate 75, U.S. Route 127, M-72 and M-93.(Wikipedia)

The city of Grayling is considered a rural enviroment (Wikipedia)

The median income in Grayling for a houshold was $30,417 according to the 2022 data.
This is below the state median income of $66,167 at that same time (U.S. Census Bureau)
