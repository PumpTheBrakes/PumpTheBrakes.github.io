City of Marquette Michigan

Here are some facts about the city of Marquette.

City Pop. in 2020 was 20,609.(U.S. Census Bureau)

The city of Marquette was incorporated in 1871. (wikipedia contributors)

Marquette is located in Michigans upper peninsula on the shore of Lake Superior.

The city of Marquette is considered a rural enviroment (Wikipedia contributors)

The median income in Marquette for a houshold was $50,257 according to the 2022 data.
This is below the state median income of $66,167 at that same time (Marquette, Michigan)
