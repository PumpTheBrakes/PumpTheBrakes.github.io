Taniti Restaurants

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Taniti Offers many options for Eaterys with an abundance of fresh seafood.


• Local fish and rice resturaunts include the following:
  1. Joe's fish shack
  2. Fish are Fresh
  3. Catch of the Day
  4. Surf and Turf
  5. Fresh over Rice
• American Style classics include the following
  1. Burgers and Beers
  2. Tenderloins
  3. SteakHouse
• Pan-Asian is served at the following

  1. The Hot Pan
  2. The Asian Place
