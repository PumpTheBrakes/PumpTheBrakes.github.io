Things to do around the Island

The island of Taniti offers many options for entertainment such as
Beaches, rainforest, volcano and local history museums among other
fun activities listed below

Taniti Offers many Fun activities in Merriton Landing on the North side of Yellow Leaf Bay found below.
• Chartered fishing tours starting at $350 for half day
• Snorkeling starts at $75
• Abundance of pubs and dancing at a New Night club
• Helicopter rides starting at $250
• Zip lining in the rainforest starts at $50.
